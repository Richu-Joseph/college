St.Antony's College,Peruvanthanam, KK Road, Idukki District,685532

St. Antony's College is a self-financing institution located in the town of Peruvanthanam (Idukki district), which is famous for its Flora and Fauna. It was established in 2013. The College is affiliated to Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam. The College offers Undergraduate Courses in Arts, Science, Commerce and Management. Also, it offers postgraduate courses in commerce.

Faculty

Our faculty consists of highly qualified, well experienced and dedicated professionals. Faculty members enhance the learning environment through academic advising, effective teaching, scholarly activities, counseling of students etc… They recognize individual needs of students and provide learning opportunity for students by developing efficient and equitable procedures for academic performances.
